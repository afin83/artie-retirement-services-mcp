# Presentation Notes

## Core narrative

The strongest version of the story is not:

> ART builds an AI chatbot.

It is:

> ART becomes the trusted retirement service that AI assistants can ask.

## Before and after

Today:

> Members have to find the right ART channel, page, tool, calculator, or person.

Future:

> Members ask a life question where they already are, and ARTie provides trusted retirement capability inside that conversation.

## Memorable line

> The strategic question is not whether ART should build another chatbot. It is whether ART should be present when members ask AI the most important retirement questions of their lives.

## Demo framing

Say:

> This is synthetic data and a concept experience, but the permission moment is real: ChatGPT is connecting to an ARTie service inside the ChatGPT experience.

Do not say:

> This is production-ready.

Do not spend time on:

- MCP
- APIs
- tunnels
- hosting
- implementation details

## Executive takeaway

If members increasingly ask AI assistants for retirement help, the assistant may become the relationship layer.

ART has a choice:

- remain a destination members must navigate to, or
- become a trusted capability available inside those AI-led retirement moments.
